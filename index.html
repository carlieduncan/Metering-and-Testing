ABOUT US

PROVIDING THE BEST PRODUCTION WELL TESTING SERVICES & RENTAL EQUIPMENT IN THE PERMIAN BASIN & BEYOND.

Since 1961, Metering & Testing Services, Inc. has been at the forefront of the oil and gas industry.

Our commitment to excellence in all we do, combined with a fleet of innovative production well testing equipment allows our customers’ operations to run effectively and efficiently.

We stand by our roots as a family-owned business and we stand by our customers with a commitment to unparalleled levels of customer service.

With a combined knowledge of over a hundred years of industry experience, our services have proven to be time-tested.

SAFETY

At Metering & Testing Services, Inc. we take health, safety and environmental protection very seriously and believe that a comprehensive HSE policy is integral to providing quality service.


Metering & Testing Services, Inc. reports health and safety data to both ISNetworld and PEC/Premier Safety Management System regularly.

We recently received an “A” RAVS Plus Ranking from ISNetworld. RAVS Plus is an implementation assessment of contractors’ written health, safety and environmental program.

We also partner with Axiom Medical Consulting, LLC specializing in total medical management services in the fields of workers' compensation, disability case management and OSHA-mandated medical programs.
